Top Smoking Recipes

Classic Texas Brisket

  • Rub: Salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • Smoke Temp: 225°F
  • Time: 10–12 hours

Trim the brisket, apply rub, and let it sit overnight. Smoke fat side up, spritz with apple cider vinegar every few hours. Wrap in butcher paper at the stall (~160°F internal). Rest for 1 hour before slicing.

Memphis Dry Rub Ribs

  • Rub: Paprika, brown sugar, chili powder, onion powder
  • Smoke Temp: 225°F
  • Time: 5–6 hours (3-2-1 method)

Remove membrane, apply rub generously. Smoke unwrapped 3 hours, then wrap in foil with butter for 2 hours. Unwrap and return to smoker to finish with a glaze or more rub.

Spicy Smoked Wings

  • Seasoning: Cayenne, paprika, garlic powder, salt
  • Smoke Temp: 250°F
  • Time: 2 hours

Pat wings dry and season evenly. Smoke at 250°F until internal temp reaches 175°F. For extra crisp, finish on grill or under broiler. Toss in spicy butter or hot sauce if desired.

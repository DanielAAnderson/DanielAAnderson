Types of Smokers

Offset

Classic wood-burning smoker with strong smoky flavor, great for large cuts.

Pellet

Electric-powered and user-friendly, uses wood pellets for clean, even smoke.

Electric

Ideal for beginners. Plug in, set the temp, and let it go. Minimal oversight needed.

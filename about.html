About Smokin' Foods

Smokin' Foods was founded by a small crew of backyard barbecue lovers who believed great smoked meat should be accessible to everyone—not just the pros. We started sharing tips and techniques online and quickly grew into a community of passionate pitmasters.

What We Teach

  • Choosing the right wood for your meat
  • Mastering the perfect smoke ring
  • Controlling temperature for consistent results
  • Knowing when to wrap and when to rest
  • How different smokers impact flavor

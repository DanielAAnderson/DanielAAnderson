Welcome to Forever Lockedin! This project is created for educational purposes only as part of a class assignment. Please read the following carefully:

  • You will not share your password or account details.
  • 1. About the App
    • This app allows users to create temporary text and image posts.
    • Posts and images are not stored on any server.
    • All data is stored locally in your browser (on your own device).
    • No one else can see your posts — they are not shared publicly or with other users.
  • 2. User Accounts
    • We use Firebase to handle email and password authentication.
    • This is only used to verify identity — no other personal data is stored or used.
  • 3. Data Storage
    • All posts and uploaded images are saved only in your browser (local storage).
    • When you refresh or close the page, all posts are deleted and cannot be recovered.
    • No data is uploaded to a database or shared externally.
  • 4. Limitations
    • Some buttons or features may appear in the app but do not currently function.
    • The app is incomplete and not intended for real-world use.
  • 5. Privacy
    • We do not collect or store any personal information other than email login via Firebase.
    • Your data stays on your device and is not accessed by anyone else.
  • 6. Disclaimer
    • This app is for educational demonstration purposes only.
    • We are not responsible for any loss of data due to app limitations, refreshes, or browser behavior.
  • By using this app, you acknowledge that it is a class project and agree to the temporary, local-only nature of the content.
